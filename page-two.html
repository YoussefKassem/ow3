What has Trump built so far?

The eight Prototype walls. To capture them, the photographer climbed a spur of the Laguna Mountains.

In this way he could look beyond the old boundary of rusty corrugated iron. An unpaved piece of road, waste, two car wrecks. The way we think we know Mexico.
